Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2025

All sales of digital products are final. Because our Guides are delivered instantly as digital downloads, we are unable to offer refunds once a purchase has been made and the file has been delivered. Please read the full policy below.

Why we do not offer refunds

Self Beacon sells digital products — downloadable PDF guides delivered immediately upon purchase. Unlike physical goods, digital files cannot be returned once accessed. Once a Guide has been downloaded or made available for download, the transaction is complete.

We encourage you to take the free burnout assessment before purchasing to confirm your archetype and ensure you are buying the right Guide for your situation. The assessment is completely free and available without any purchase or registration requirement.

What is covered — and what is not

We will help you if: You completed a purchase but did not receive your download link, the download link is broken or expired, or you received the wrong Guide due to a technical error. Contact us within 7 days and we will resend your product at no charge.

We cannot process refunds if: You changed your mind after downloading, you felt the Guide was not right for your situation, you purchased the wrong archetype, your circumstances changed, or you did not use the Guide.

Before you buy

We want you to be confident in your purchase. Before completing any purchase, we recommend:

  • Taking the free assessment at selfbeacon.com to identify your archetype
  • Reading the full product description on the purchase page
  • Reviewing what is included in each Guide on our website
  • Emailing us at wecare@selfbeacon.com if you have any questions before purchasing

Technical delivery issues

If you experience a technical problem receiving your Guide — such as a failed download, a corrupted file, or a link that does not work — contact us at wecare@selfbeacon.com within 7 days of your purchase. Include your order number and a description of the issue. We will resend your Guide within 24 hours.

Chargebacks and payment disputes

By completing a purchase, you agree to this refund policy and acknowledge that initiating a chargeback or payment dispute for a delivered digital product may constitute fraud. If you have a genuine concern about your purchase, please contact us directly at wecare@selfbeacon.com before raising a dispute with your payment provider. We are always willing to work with you to resolve legitimate issues.

Consumer rights

Nothing in this policy affects your statutory rights under applicable consumer protection laws. In some jurisdictions, consumers may have additional rights regarding digital products. If you believe you have statutory rights that apply to your situation, please contact us.
