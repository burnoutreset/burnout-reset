Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

The short version: We do not collect personal information to sell it. The free assessment runs entirely in your browser. We only collect what we need to deliver your purchase and communicate with you.

1. Who we are

Self Beacon ("we", "us", "our") operates the website selfbeacon.com and sells digital wellness guides. Our contact email is wecare@selfbeacon.com.

2. Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Purchase information: When you buy a guide through Payhip, Payhip collects your email address and payment details. We receive your email address so we can deliver your product and communicate with you about your purchase.
  • Contact information: If you email us or submit our affiliate application form, we collect your name, email address, and any information you include in your message.

Information collected automatically

  • Assessment data: The free burnout assessment runs entirely in your browser. Your answers, emotions, and inventory responses are never transmitted to our servers. Nothing from the assessment is stored or shared.
  • Usage data: Like most websites, we may collect basic analytics such as pages visited, browser type, and approximate location through standard web analytics tools. This data is aggregated and anonymised.
  • Cookies: We may use essential cookies to enable basic site functionality. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

3. How we use your information

  • To deliver your purchased digital product
  • To respond to your support requests and enquiries
  • To process affiliate applications and manage affiliate relationships
  • To improve our products and website
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Payhip — payment processing and digital product delivery. See payhip.com/privacy
  • Netlify — website hosting and serverless functions
  • GitHub Pages — hosting of the assessment application
  • Google Fonts — typography (loaded from Google's CDN)

When you make a purchase, Payhip's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment information. We do not store or have access to your payment card details.

5. Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as necessary to fulfil your purchase and any subsequent support requests. If you request deletion of your data, we will comply within 30 days unless we are required to retain it for legal or tax purposes.

6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Object to or restrict processing of your information
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at wecare@selfbeacon.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and to contact us immediately if you suspect any unauthorised use of your information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at: wecare@selfbeacon.com
